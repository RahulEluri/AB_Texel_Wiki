# Frequently Asked Questions

## Access & Permissions

**Q: How do I request access to a report?**  
A: Contact the Data Administration team or your manager. See [Access & Permissions](03-access-permissions.md) for details.

**Q: Can I share reports with external users?**  
A: External sharing requires security approval. Contact [support@abtexel.com](mailto:support@abtexel.com) to request.

**Q: How often are permissions reviewed?**  
A: User permissions are reviewed quarterly and when roles change.

## Data & Refresh

**Q: When is data refreshed?**  
A: Data refresh schedules vary by report. See [Data Sources & Refresh Schedule](02-data-sources.md).

**Q: Why is my report data delayed?**  
A: Check [Data Sources & Refresh Schedule](02-data-sources.md) for known delays and SLAs.

**Q: Can I get real-time data?**  
A: Most reports refresh daily. BigMile fleet data updates hourly. Some metrics update real-time. Contact support for specific needs.

## Reports

**Q: Which report should I use for [specific need]?**  
A: Browse the [Reports - Power BI](reports/00-reports-overview.md) section or [Contact & Support](10-contact-support.md) for recommendations.

**Q: Can I export report data?**  
A: Export permissions depend on your role. See [Access & Permissions](03-access-permissions.md).

**Q: How do I schedule automated report delivery?**  
A: Contact your report owner or support team to set up scheduled deliveries.

## Technical

**Q: What if I find incorrect data in a report?**  
A: Report the issue to [support@abtexel.com](mailto:support@abtexel.com) with:
- Report name
- Data that looks incorrect
- Expected value
- Steps to reproduce

**Q: Why can't I access a report?**  
A: Possible causes:
1. You don't have permission (see [Access & Permissions](03-access-permissions.md))
2. System maintenance is in progress
3. Technical issue - contact support

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**  
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions). Contact IT for other browsers.

## General

**Q: Where can I provide feedback or suggestions?**  
A: Contact the Data Team at [support@abtexel.com](mailto:support@abtexel.com). We value your input!

**Q: Is there training available?**  
A: Yes! Contact the Data Team to arrange training sessions.

**Q: Who should I contact for more help?**  
A: See [Contact & Support](10-contact-support.md) for all contact information.
