# Power BI Reports

## Available Reports

AB Texel provides a comprehensive suite of Power BI reports covering key business areas. Browse the reports below or contact the analytics team for recommendations.

## Report Categories

### Financial Reports
- **[Financial Operations](01-financial-operations.md)** - Accounting, revenue, expenses, and general ledger analysis

### Operational Reports
- **[Fleet Hours](02-fleet-hours.md)** - Vehicle utilization, driver hours, and fleet efficiency metrics
- **[Fuel Reporting](04-fuel-reporting.md)** - Fuel consumption, costs, and efficiency analysis

### Customer Reports
- **[Customer Reporting](03-customer-reporting.md)** - Customer performance, accounts, and engagement metrics

### System Monitoring
- **[Anomaly Reporting](05-anomaly-reporting.md)** - System alerts, data quality issues, and anomalies

## Report Permissions

Your access to reports depends on your role. See [Access & Permissions](../03-access-permissions.md) for details.

**Need access?** Contact [support@abtexel.com](mailto:support@abtexel.com)

## Report Performance

All reports are optimized for performance with typical load times under 5 seconds. If you experience slowness, try:
1. Clearing your browser cache
2. Using a supported browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
3. Contacting support if issues persist
