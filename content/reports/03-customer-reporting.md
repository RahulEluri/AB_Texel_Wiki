# Customer Reporting Report

## Overview

The Customer Reporting dashboard provides insights into customer performance, account health, engagement, and profitability metrics.

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Description | Update Frequency |
|--------|-------------|------------------|
| Customer Count | Total number of active customers | Daily |
| Revenue per Customer | Average revenue generated | Daily |
| Customer Satisfaction | NPS and satisfaction scores | Weekly |
| Customer Growth | New customers acquired | Daily |
| Churn Rate | Customers lost | Weekly |
| Lifetime Value | Predicted total customer value | Monthly |

## Report Sections

### Customer Overview
- Total customer count and trends
- Revenue distribution by customer
- Geographic distribution
- Customer segmentation

### Account Health
- Active vs. inactive accounts
- Account age analysis
- Revenue trends by account
- Engagement metrics

### Performance Analysis
- Top performing customers
- Low-performing accounts requiring attention
- Customer tier analysis
- Revenue growth by segment

### Engagement Metrics
- Contact frequency
- Support ticket volume
- Feature adoption
- Renewal likelihood

## How to Use

1. **Access:** Log in to Power BI and navigate to Customer Reporting
2. **Filter:** Select date range, customer segment, or region
3. **Analyze:** View customer trends and performance
4. **Export:** Download customer lists or reports

## Key Features

- Customer-level drill-down
- Segmentation by type and size
- Engagement scoring
- Predictive analytics
- Export capabilities
