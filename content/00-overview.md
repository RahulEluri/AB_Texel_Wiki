# AB Texel Reporting Overview

## Welcome to the AB Texel Wiki

This wiki serves as a comprehensive guide to AB Texel's reporting system, data sources, and available reports. Whether you're a new user or a seasoned analyst, this documentation will help you navigate our reporting infrastructure and find the information you need.

## What You'll Find Here

### Core Documentation
- **Reporting Process & Ownership** - Understand how reports are created and maintained
- **Data Sources & Refresh Schedule** - Learn where data comes from and when it's updated
- **Access & Permissions** - Get information about user roles and access levels

### Available Reports
- **Power BI Reports** - Interactive dashboards for financial, fleet, customer, and fuel data
- **Excel Reporting** - Client-facing reports and exports
- **Anomaly Reporting** - System monitoring and alerts

### System Information
- **System Integration** - Details on CBS & BigMile integrations
- **Glossary** - Common terminology and definitions
- **FAQs** - Frequently asked questions
