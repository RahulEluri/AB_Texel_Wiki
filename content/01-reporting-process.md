# Reporting Process & Ownership

## Overview

This document outlines the reporting process at AB Texel, including the workflow, roles, responsibilities, and approval procedures.

## Reporting Workflow

### Step 1: Report Request
- Reports are requested through the standard change request process
- Requests are logged in the system and assigned a tracking ID
- Initial review and feasibility assessment is conducted

### Step 2: Development & Design
- Report is designed based on requirements
- Data sources are identified and validated
- Initial development begins in a development environment

### Step 3: Testing & Validation
- Reports are tested with sample data
- Accuracy and performance are verified
- Stakeholder review and feedback is gathered

### Step 4: Deployment
- Reports are deployed to production
- Documentation is updated
- Users are notified of availability

### Step 5: Monitoring & Maintenance
- Reports are monitored for performance issues
- Data refresh schedules are maintained
- Updates and patches are applied as needed

## Team Roles & Responsibilities

### Report Owner
- Maintains report accuracy and relevance
- Responds to user questions and support requests
- Manages report updates and enhancements
- Oversees data source validation

### Data Analyst
- Develops and tests reports
- Validates data integrity
- Optimizes report performance
- Provides technical support

### Data Administrator
- Manages system access and permissions
- Ensures data security and compliance
- Monitors data refresh schedules
- Handles system integrations

### Business Owner
- Defines reporting requirements
- Approves report design and outputs
- Ensures reports meet business needs
- Communicates with end users
