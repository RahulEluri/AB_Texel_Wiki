## Incident Reporting

### Reporting a System Issue

1. **Document the problem:**
   - What were you trying to do?
   - What happened instead?
   - When did it start?
   - Any error messages?

2. **Contact support** with:
   - Issue description
   - Report/system name
   - Steps to reproduce
   - Screenshots (if applicable)
   - Urgency level (Critical/High/Medium/Low)

### Severity Levels

| Severity | Impact | Response Time | Example |
|----------|--------|----------------|---------|
| Critical | System down, data loss risk | 1 hour | Reports completely unavailable |
| High | Major functionality broken | 4 hours | Cannot access key reports |
| Medium | Partial functionality issue | 8 hours | Report showing incorrect data |
| Low | Minor issues, workaround exists | 24-48 hours | Display formatting issue |
